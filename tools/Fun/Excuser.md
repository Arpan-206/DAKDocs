# Excuser
It is a fun little tool to make various excuses related to the situation.

# Acceptable Inputs for category
- *Blank* or Random
- Family
- Office
- Children
- College
- Party

*Note- Anything other than above mentioned inputs will simply be treated as a Blank Input.*
